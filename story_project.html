What If Story Project

This project was a passion project from last year, created with a classmate. We explored the concept of "what if our thoughts were shown." Over a month and a half, we put immense effort into voice acting, recording reference clips with a professional video camera, and editing, which was my primary role. The final product is a compelling narrative video, showcasing our creativity and collaborative skills.
