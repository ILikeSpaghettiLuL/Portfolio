UI/UX Design for School Chair

In this unique design-focused course, I ventured into UI/UX principles, moving away from the typical business-centric topics. I developed a deep understanding of Figma, learning key design principles such as color theory, typography, and the effective use of whitespace. Although the project presented initial challenges, I designed a minimalist, black-and-white interface for a school chair concept, which demonstrated my growing expertise in the field. The end result was both sleek and functional, reinforcing my confidence in pursuing student jobs related to UI/UX design in the future.
